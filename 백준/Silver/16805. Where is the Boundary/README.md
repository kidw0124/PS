### 문제 설명

<p>An island country JAGAN in a certain planet is very long and narrow, and extends east and west. This long country is said to consist of two major cultural areas — the eastern and the western. Regions in the east tend to have the eastern cultural features and regions in the west tend to have the western cultural features, but, of course, the boundary between the two cultural areas is not clear, which has been an issue.</p>

<p>You are given an assignment estimating the boundary using a given data set.</p>
